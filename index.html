The Challenge

Exercise is Medicine. But It's Not Yet Part of the System.

Modern healthcare excels at preventing acute death. It continues to struggle with prevention, chronic disease, frailty, and functional decline. Exercise is one of the most powerful interventions for long-term health, yet it remains poorly operationalized in clinical care.
